Fashion Show Poster

The Task: Kildress Wolfe Tones GAC needed a captivating poster for their fundraising Fashion Show. Their colours were to be within the poster, as should their crest and event details.

The Solution: The club colours are not the most visually appealing - green, white and gold so I decided to work with a more limited palette of just gold and white. I used clipping masks in order to create this strong geometric representation. I think colour, shape and type all combine in the piece to create an aesthetically appealing piece.

Granda's Old Tractor

The Task: Use colouring pencils and pens to draw something symbolic of my family.

The Solution: I drew my Granda's old tractor as not only a representation of him but also my family history as a whole on both sides. To me, farming is a job and a lifestyle that requires hard-work and perservence. I see this tractor as a symbol of that.

Back to the Future Movie Poster

The Task: Create a modernism inspired movie poster for your favourite movies.

The Solution: I used symbolic imagery to convey the narrative and feeling of Back to the Future. I used a gradient to create the lines of fire that are left by the DeLorean. I also used strong typography, in keeping with the modernist style.
